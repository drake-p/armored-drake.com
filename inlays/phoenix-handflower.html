Phoenix Inlay Handflower

This served as my entry into the second-ever M.A.I.L. theme contest, "Mythical Creatures."  There were a total of four entries, and this one tied for first with sakredchao's Balrog sculpture.

I spent about a week fiddling around with the basic image before I was happy enough to start weaving it.  Here is the image that I ended up using:


Rings:

European 4:1 weave
