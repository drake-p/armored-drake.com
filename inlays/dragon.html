Dragon Inlay

One of my first inlays.  So, yeah, from maybe my second year of mailling, long before I learned how to use a dense ring size for inlays.

It's not my design, mind you, although I wish I could have been that good at the time.  The pattern was found here.  My finished piece is slightly different from the pattern because I ran out of rings or room or something; anyway my dragon only has one wing.  Probably makes it look even dumber than it already does with the ridiculous ring size.  At the time, though, I thought this inlay was just about the coolest thing since refrigerators.

Rings:

20 ga 5/32" ID
Sapphire, gold, and silver craft wire (enameled copper)
European 4:1
