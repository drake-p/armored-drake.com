Water Bottle

Heh, okay, so this is a little frivolous.  But see, in my house, there are four people, all of whom have water bottles, and all of said bottles are identical.  So we each have to figure out some way to put an identifying marker on our particular water bottle.

Put our names on them, you say?  Not in this house!  Way too simple!

So this is my water bottle.  I added a Half Persian chain with neoprene around the neck, and wrote "Warning: Dihydrogen Monoxide" around it.  If you don't know about Dihydrogen Monoxide, well, try a Google search, it ought to turn up a few things.  (And if you can't figure out what dihydrogen monoxide is, the fact that I put it on my water bottle -- I repeat, *water* ahem *water* bottle -- should be pretty telling).

Rings:

19 ga 3/16" ID
Heat-colored stainless steel
16 ga 1/4" ID
White neoprene (so that I can get it over the neck of the bottle)
In the Half Persian weave.
