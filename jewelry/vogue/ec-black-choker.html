Black Gothic Choker:

This choker was inspired by one that I saw in the movie of The Phantom of the Opera (with Gerard Butler and Emmy Rossum).  It's just a fleeting glimpse, you only get a couple shots of it in the movie, so I can't really say how close this one is to the original in the movie.  But I think it looks pretty good on its own merit.

It is made of three tiers of scalloped chains on a simple 3-row-wide strip of European 4:1.  There are five jet beads at the intervals.  It has no clasp, as it is meant to be tied with a ribbon.

Rings:

18 ga 5/32" ID
20 ga 1/8" ID
Black craft wire
In the European 4:1 and 1-1 chain weaves.
