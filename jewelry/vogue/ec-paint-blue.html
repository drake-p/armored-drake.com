Paint It Blue!

Mmm...my favoritest color ever.  I started fooling around with a strip of blue and black Dragonscale, then -- in one of those "Oh, what if!" moments -- held it up next to a cabochon I had been trying to work with.  The colors were an absolutely perfect match.  Well, that solved both the problem of what to do with the chainmaille and what to do with the cabochon.  Then I noticed a blue bracelet that I had made previously, and realized that it used the same color of EC as the pendant did.  So once I had both a pendant and a bracelet, I needed earrings -- no problem!  And this is the set that resulted.  Yes, it is in a martini glass.
