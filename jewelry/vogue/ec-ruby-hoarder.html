Ruby Hoarder

She has sinuous scales of red and silver, a ferocious temperament, and a penchant for all things shiny and valuable.  She'll take silver, gold, or gemstones to hoard in her lair, but her favorites are the bright glistening red of rubies.

You've heard of Smaug, you've perhaps heard of Beowulf's killer, but here now is a late comer: the serpentine figure of the Ruby Hoarder.
