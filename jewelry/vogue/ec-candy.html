Candy bracelet

I still haven't decided whether I like this one or not.  I just can't get over the feeling that I'm wearing a bag of Skittles on my wrist.

The main point was to use up a bunch of leftover beads and drops, which goal was at least effectively achieved.

Rings:

20 ga 7/64" ID
Burgundy, red, silvered-tangerine, silvered-peach, and silvered-lemon enameled copper
European 4:1 woven on the bias (the edges go diagonally across the weave)
4mm Swarovski bicones (lemon or citrus or something like that), unknown stats on the red and orange dagger beads.
Wire wrap on the beads was done with 24 ga silver-plate wire.
