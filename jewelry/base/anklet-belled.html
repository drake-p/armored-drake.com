Belled Anklet

My first wares aimed at the bellydancing crowd.  I scavenged the bells from an old necklace or something that I found, and put them on a new chain.  It makes a nice sound, and I like how shiny the bright aluminum is.

Rings:

16 ga 1/4" ID
Bright Aluminum (alloy 5356)
18 ga 3/16" ID
Plain aluminum
In the Half Persian 3:1 weave, with European 4:1 dags.
