Thanksgiving Set

This was the product of some cool beads that I'd had for ages, some silver that I didn't know what to do with, and a few copper rings that got thrown into the mix.  I started it on the first day of my Thanksgiving break.  At first, I just intended it to be a silver chain with some beads, but then I happened to use a few copper rings for placeholders and thought, hey, those colors look pretty cool together!  Plus, I wasn't going to have enough silver to do it entirely in one metal.

Once I finished the bracelet, earrings were a given, and I wore the whole shebang for Thanksgiving dinner.

Rings:

18 ga 5/32" ID
Sterling Silver and Copper
In the Byzantine weave, with three connectors (see, the chain has extra stuffing even...)
