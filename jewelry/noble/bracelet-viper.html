Viper Bracelet

Sterling and titanium: the two best metals in the world (in my opinion), put together in this bracelet that looks and moves like a serpent.  The silver is beautifully clear and white, while the titanium adds an earthy grey tone and the novelty of space-age technology.

It was a bit of a challenge to pull off, as titanium is considerably harder than silver.  Because of this, if I wind the two metals on the same size mandrel, the titanium will tend to expand more (it has more springback), resulting in a larger diameter ring.  To avoid this, I had to wind the silver on a 3/16" mandrel, and the titanium on a 5/32" one.
