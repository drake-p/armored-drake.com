Scalloped Choker

Another experiment with micromaille, fun.  I think the scallops on this are the same ring size as the chains on my Amethyst Fringe choker, maybe slightly bigger.

Lots of rings, lots of time, lots of work -- that's the mailler's creed right there.

Rings:

18 and 20 ga 5/32" ID
24 ga, ID of 14 ga wire
Silver-plated and sterling silver
In the European 4:1 and Byzantine weaves.
