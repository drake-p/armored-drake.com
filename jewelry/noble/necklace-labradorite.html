Labradorite Choker

Utterly simple and simply elegant.

My first Half Persian-wrapped cabochon, which is not even, technically, a cabochon.  It is a top-drilled oval piece of labradorite (yes, that's really the name -- I think it comes from the same place as the dog breed), but I preferred wrapping it with a chain to somehow mounting it on a wire.  The chain fits snugly over the stone, and is held in place with a layer of clear epoxy around the edges.

The piece is laced in the back with a full yard of dark blue silk cord.  See below for modeled photos.

Rings:
18 ga (0.045") 5/32" ID
20 ga 9/64" ID
Sterling Silver
European 4:1 and Half Persian 3:1
