Little Purse

A very, very small purse.  It is mostly in gold-colored artistic wire, with a strip of silver color across the middle.  It would be perfect for a New Year's Eve party, considering size and glittery aspect.

An interesting thing to note:  I think that there are in fact more links in the strap than there are in the purse itself, or else it's very close to the same number.

Below the purse is a picture of the whiteboard at my house after I had finished the project.  It had turned out to take much longer than I had originally anticipated, and, well, I was glad to be done.

Rings:

20 ga 5/32" ID
Gold and Silver Artistic Wire
In the European 4:1 and Boxchain weaves.
