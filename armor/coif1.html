First Coif

This was my second chainmail project ever, a coif with an open front and decorative trim.  It is not an armor-class piece -- it may just hold up to a metal ruler, though I wouldn't count on it --  but it is very good for Halloween and other such events.

Rings:
16 ga. 3/8" ID
Etched Aluminum with Bronze trim
In the European 4:1 expanding circle weave.
