Welcome to my gallery.

This site archives photos of just about every chainmaille project I've ever completed that I deemed presentable, as well as information I've gathered about the craft. Please have a look around, enjoy the eye candy, and maybe, if I've done well, learn something.

Although this site is updated only infrequently, I am now keeping a blog, The Poiesis Journal, mostly about my new adventures in bookbinding. I also have several of the items on this site available in my Etsy shop. Enjoy.
